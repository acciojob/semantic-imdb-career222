3 Idiots is a comedy-drama film that follows the lives of three engineering students. The movie highlights friendship, education, and the importance of following your passion instead of blindly chasing success.

Actors

Aamir Khan, R. Madhavan, Sharman Joshi, Kareena Kapoor, and Boman Irani played the lead roles in the movie.
